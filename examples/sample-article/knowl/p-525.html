You might also wish to provide a prefix to a cross-reference and have it incorporated into the text of what you would click on in an electronic version. So if you make an xref with some content, then that content will prefix the cross-reference within the clickable/pokeable text and be attached with a non-breaking space. This xref content totally overrides any prefix that might happen otherwise. So the name of an item (e.g. “corollary”) could be replaced, and if you make a cross-reference with a title as the clickable, then that text can be replaced also. An example:

  • A grand result: Major Corollary 4.1

  • A grand result: a nice corollary
