Lemma3.5Every sequence has a monotonic subsequence

Let \(s_n\) be a sequence of real numbers. Then there exists a sequence of natural numbers

\begin{equation*} n_1 \lt n_2 \lt n_3 \lt \cdots \end{equation*}

such that the sequence

\begin{equation*} s_{n_1}, s_{n_2}, s_{n_3}, \ldots \end{equation*}

is a monotonic sequence.

(Note, we will later call this an example of a subsequence of \(s_n\text{.}\))
