Proposition3.3.7

A subset \(H\) of \(G\) is a subgroup if and only if it satisfies the following conditions.

  1. The identity \(e\) of \(G\) is in \(H\text{.}\)

  2. If \(h_1, h_2 \in H\text{,}\) then \(h_1h_2 \in H\text{.}\)

  3. If \(h \in H\text{,}\) then \(h^{-1} \in H\text{.}\)
